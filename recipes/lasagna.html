How to make Lasagna

Description

Lasagne, singular lasagna, are a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made of very wide, flat sheets.Typically cooked pasta is assembled with the other ingredients and then baked in an oven. The resulting baked pasta is cut into single-serving square portions.
